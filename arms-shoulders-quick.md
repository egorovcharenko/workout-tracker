# Arms & Shoulders — Quick Session (~15 min)

| # | Exercise | Sets × Reps | Notes |
|---|----------|-------------|-------|
| 1 | Overhead dumbbell press | 3 × 10-12 | Seated or standing |
| 2 | Dumbbell bicep curls | 3 × 10-12 | Superset with #3 |
| 3 | Overhead tricep extension | 3 × 10-12 | Single DB, both hands |

**Rest:** 60-90 sec (only after completing each superset of #2 + #3)

**Warm-up:** A few light sets to warm up elbows and wrists before starting.
